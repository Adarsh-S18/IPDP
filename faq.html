Frequently Asked Questions

1. Shall I get a job if I attend the program?

All-time question. We help you get ready for a job and search for a job in a better way. We provide long-term placement support. There is no guaranteed job since hiring depends on how employable you are! We make you more employable to land your dream job!

2. Does an online program really work?

Yes! We prefer a small batch with less than 10 participants; we give everyone personal attention. We insist participants keep the camera on. We host lots of fun activities to boost teamwork. We always stay keen to keep the team spirit high. We keep ourselves available at all times to ensure participants results.

3. Is your google review fake or manipulated?

No! 100%; it is given by our program participants and clients only. If you are not convinced, you can search their LinkedIn or social media profiles or directly ask them! We are pretty sure you will get only good responses!

4. Can I get pre-recorded classes?

There is no scope for it. Mostly every day we have group discussion sessions, and pre-recorded videos won't help you! To improve soft skills, you need to interact, be involved, and evolve. For the informative learning sessions, if you could not attend them for a genuine reason, we would organize them again personally for you. We take that additional effort because one-on-one personalized interactions will yield a higher-quality result.

5. Will it be a hectic schedule for students and working professionals?

Never! The only thing you should have is a good network facility and 1 to 1.15 hours every weekday for 5 weeks (program duration). There will be some work to be done from your end, which may take an average of 15 minutes in some days. You will enjoy the process, and you will learn to manage your time better!

6. I already have a resume and LinkedIn profile, so what is the need to know more about them?

You can test the quality of your resume and LinkedIn from a team with recruitment experience. The same team will help you learn about the resume on LinkedIn in a better way from a recruiter's perspective.

7. What support does one get after the program?

We have extended support. Once you are a Certified Intern-Preneur, you are family to us! You can reach out to us for any help related to careers and recruitment. We also provide long-term support for placement. We maintain a tribe community where we focus on providing value additions through helpful sessions and different kinds of programs to support them and keep them upskilled themselves.

8. How can I improve my confidence through an online program?

This program gives you a comfortable space to break your comfort zone! You will be in a small team, and you can try everything that inhibits you. If you listen, talk, say your opinions, and lead a team, you can try everything in a friendly, non-judgmental space. When you try, you learn and grow. Definitely, you can boost your confidence. We have seen small to massive changes in terms of confidence among participants these days! and we will assist your journey throughout.

9. Have seen many skill development programs, so how do you differentiate from others?

Our program is simple, goal-oriented, and an experiential learning program. This allows learners to deepen their understanding of the content subject matter and cultivate skills that can be applied in various practice environments or employment settings. Participants feel empowered to use their knowledge and skills in contexts that will aid in their career development. We keep everything simple and sensible, and we also offer individual attention to our participants.
